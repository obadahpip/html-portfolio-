About Me

Hi, I’m Obadah — an ambitious entrepreneur, AI enthusiast, and business leader driven by innovation, impact, and growth.

Ever since I was young, I’ve had a deep passion for solving problems and building things that make a difference. I launched my first venture in trading education at 20, sharing practical strategies and insights that helped hundreds improve their financial literacy. That project, now known as MTA (Millionaire Trading Academy), grew into a powerful platform with over 45 hours of free content, multiple paid products, and a loyal community.

But I didn’t stop there.

I’ve since stepped into executive leadership, taking on the role of General Manager at Sadida Agricultural Company in Chad, where I’m building systems to transform it into a global agribusiness. From ERP systems like Odoo to hands-on operations in sesame processing and export, I’m bridging tech, trade, and strategy.

I’m also constantly learning — from French and Python to gold mining and automation — because I believe curiosity is the fuel for greatness.

This site is where I share my journey, my ideas, and the projects I’m proud of. Whether you’re here to collaborate, connect, or just explore, I’m glad you stopped by.
