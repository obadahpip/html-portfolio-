The Best Movies According to Obadah

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Grand Budapest Hotel

This comedy, directed by Wes Anderson, follows the adventures of a legendary concierge and his lobby boy at a famous European hotel between the wars, involving a stolen Renaissance painting and a battle for family fortune.

Mad Max: Fury Road

This high-octane action film is set in a post-apocalyptic wasteland, where Max teams up with Furiosa to flee from a tyrannical warlord and his army, leading to an epic road battle.

Inception

This mind-bending thriller by Christopher Nolan dives into the world of dreams, where a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious is given a chance to have his past crimes forgiven if he can successfully plant an idea into someone's mind.
